Team Hermetica - Water Purification

“Pure Water” is the world's first and foremost medicine, but water supplies are severely polluted, posing a serious threat. So, Team Hermetica has come up with a revolutionary technique of Water Purification using TiO2 Powder and Photocatalytic Activity. We have developed a multi-step efficient solution for ultra-purification of industrial water using TiO2 powder, Silica gel and Rice Husk membranes. Hazardous hydrocarbons, dyes, microplastics, and nano pollutants such as lead and chromium are being phased out making water safe for drinking.
