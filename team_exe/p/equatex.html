Team.EXE - EquaTex

In COVID times and otherwise, handwritten mathematical answer sheets are the commonly accepted mode of evaluation. Moreover, for academic purposes, LaTex documents are a must but are also tedious to make when it comes to mathematical questions. This process is manual and requires a lot of time and effort. All that time and effort can be saved by using Computer Vision and Deep Neural Networks to automate the entire grading Process. For publishing purposes, we are aiming to create a system that converts handwritten mathematical equations to latex code.
